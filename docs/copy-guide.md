## Tone & Voice Guidelines

### General Principles
- **Friendly**: Use "vous" (FR) or "you" (EN), be conversational
- **Clear**: Avoid jargon; explain terms simply
- **Action-oriented**: Use strong verbs (Create, Generate, Upgrade)
- **Professional but human**: Balance AV industry expertise with approachability
- **Bilingual awareness**: Both FR and EN should feel native, not translated

### Example Sentences

**FR**:
- "Créez votre premier devis en 3 clics"
- "L'IA qui comprend vos tarifs"
- "Jusqu'à 5 devis/mois en Free. Illimité en Pro."

**EN**:
- "Create your first proposal in 3 clicks"
- "AI that understands your rates"
- "Up to 5 proposals/month on Free. Unlimited on Pro."
